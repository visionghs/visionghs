Postgraduates & Professionals

Vision Global Health Society

Here at Vision, we hope to create bi-directional educational opportunities that stimulate students to think critically about issues, impact communities, and grow as civically minded leaders for the future.

We would love for you to join our mission!

Highlights for Joining Vision Global Health Society

Mentor Others

Connect with students to provide career guidance, create projects that impact communities, expand on research, or share narratives..

Get Volunteers/Interns/Research Assistants

Invite students to help with projects/ do literature reviews/ learn from your experitise.

Donate

Your donation is greatly appreciated as it helps us provide more robust programming.DonateorContact Us


Give talks

Our students benefit from the insight of experienced individuals. Volunteer to give a talk at one of our conferences or opt for a virtual presentation througha video or Zoom.

Presentations & Publications

As a student organization, we understand the value that presentations and publications have in career development. Offer opportunities for students to join projects planned for publication.

 





Learn About Global Health

Through the GHLC, you will have the opportunity to learn from Harvard affiliates about many areas of global health.

Impact Communities

Students are paired with a Harvard student mentor to help guide students in creating evidence-based and cost-effective community interventions.

Grow as a Leader

Our students engage with projects that help them develop the confidence and skills that are necessary to be a successful leader.
